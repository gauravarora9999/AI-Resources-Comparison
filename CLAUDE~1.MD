# Claude Opus 4.7 vs GPT-5.5 — April 2026

## Executive Overview

**Opus 4.7 is the deepest thinker. GPT-5.5 is the broadest operator.** That single distinction drives every other decision.

Claude Opus 4.7 (16 April 2026) leads on long-horizon agentic coding, scientific reasoning, and finance — it's the model you trust to *finish* hard, multi-step work. SWE-bench Verified at 87.6%, GPQA Diamond at 94.2%, state-of-the-art Finance Agent at 64.4%, plus task budgets, finer effort controls, and built-in cybersecurity misuse filtering.

GPT-5.5 (23 April 2026) is OpenAI's natively omnimodal "super-app" model — text, image, audio, and video in one unified system. It dominates on terminal use, computer-use, long-context retrieval, and broad knowledge work, with Terminal-Bench 2.0 at 82.7% (13 points ahead of Opus), OSWorld at 78.7%, and MRCR v2 at 74% for 512K–1M context.

**Verdict:** Use both. Opus 4.7 as the reasoning core for coding agents and regulated work; GPT-5.5 as the omnimodal front-line for computer-use, multimodal automation, and broad productivity.
